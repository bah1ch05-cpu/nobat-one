# Project Proposal — Nobat One

## 1. Project Title
**Nobat One: Universal Queue Management Platform**

## 2. Team
- Team member 1
- Team member 2

## 3. Problem Statement
In many service businesses, queue management is still manual. Customers often have to:
- call the business to ask for availability
- wait physically in line
- stay uncertain about waiting time
- lose time because there is no digital visibility

This creates frustration for customers and operational inefficiency for businesses.

## 4. Proposed Solution
Nobat One is a digital queue management platform that allows customers to join queues remotely and enables businesses to manage the flow of clients through a web dashboard.

The product is not limited to restaurants. It can also be used for:
- barbershops
- clinics
- car washes
- beauty salons
- children's centers

## 5. Target Users
### Primary users
- Customers who want to avoid physical waiting
- Staff who need a clear queue dashboard

### Secondary users
- Business owners who want analytics and operational control

## 6. Value Proposition
Nobat One helps businesses reduce chaos around waiting lines while improving customer experience through transparency and convenience.

## 7. Main Features
### Customer features
- Search businesses by category
- See estimated waiting time
- Join queue online
- Track position in queue
- Receive upcoming-turn notification

### Business features
- View current queue
- Call next customer
- Manage queue rules
- Review queue history
- Track simple peak-hour analytics

## 8. Scope for Midterm MVP
The midterm MVP includes:
- customer queue join flow
- queue status page
- business queue dashboard
- basic notification simulation
- simple working prototype

## 9. Expected Outcome
A working prototype and complete software engineering documentation demonstrating requirements engineering, architecture planning, collaboration workflow, and a presentation-ready system concept.
